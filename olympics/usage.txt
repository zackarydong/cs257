NAME
	olympics - search through a olympics database

SYNOPSIS
	olympics [-h | --help] [-a | --athletes <noc>] [-m | --medals <medal>] [-y | --year <year>]

DESCRIPTION
	olympics is a search tool to find information about athletes, countries, and medal counts from our olympics database. 

OPTIONS
	-h, --help
		Prints the usage statement.

    -a, --athletes <noc>
        Lists names of all athletes whose country matches <noc>

    -m, --medals
        DEFAULT=gold
        List all the NOCs and the number of medals they have won, in decreasing order of the number of medals.

    -y, --year <year>
        Lists the top 10 athletes and their respetive countries with the most medals in the specified year
